Who are we?

Our vision is for Southern Africa to be self-sufficient in the industries and markets that we are in business by providing professional quality services of superior value that yield long-term relationships and sufficient growth.

Routica Holdings Group is focused on creating a better future for all of Africa through the product we supply and the way in which we operate, always keeping economic growth and self-sufficiency for Africa in mind.

Our vision is for Africa to become self-sufficient, with Routica Holdings Group being the most valued and reliable African supplier in the industries and markets we are in business, creating a better future for Africa and our Clients.

We will achieve our purpose and vision through purposeful and focused attention on the sourcing of our products, safety and health of all our employees, contractors and visitors at the workplace, achievement of set supply targets through the effective and efficient utilization of all resource at our disposal
