Petroleum and Oils

Our Petroleum is standard 50 PPM Diesel as per Government, as per Petroleum Products Act, 1977. We ensure all licenses are in place with our suppliers and continue to supply all our clientele that are in need. We facilitate our Petroleum supply between our supplier & the end clientele. All standard oil & diesel product ae available for purchase through us . With our partnership with our suppliers , we ensure premium quality products to all our clientele.

Personal Protective Equipment

All our PPE is up to standard as per Occupational Health and Safety Act, 2020 & Mine Health and Safety Act, 1966. This will ensure maximum comfort and protection for the individuals in use of it. Our PPE products can be used in the mining, health and car sector safely and comfortably. All quantities are available as per request.

Plant Hire & Mining Supplies

All plant hire is available at request. Our partnerships have given us the opportunity to provide our clientele with the best quality plant machinery such as TLBs, Wheel-Loaders, Excavators & Rollers. All plant hire must be requested on email. We also provide Conveyor Rollers, Hydraulic & Pneumatic Cylinders & Power Tools. Commission Fee is chargeable per transaction.

Commodities Trade

Our main commodity trades consist of Chrome Concentrate & ROM, Gold trading, Diamond trading. We are extremely passionate on raw material to finished products that may be traded from either stage dependent on the market. Our trading rules and standards are extremely strict and thus encourage each customer to follow the requirements to obtain their commodity successfully. All potential customers will be required to follow our trading rules to ensure that all our transactions together will be successful. Commission Fee is chargeable per transaction.
